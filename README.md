### Development Team Update

After several months of development, the fan-made Galgame **《败犬栖居的晴空日常》** ("The Sunny Daily Life Where Losing Heroines Reside") has completed its demo. This demo covers the content of volumes 1 to 3 of the original work and contains about 220,000 Chinese characters of text. The game includes animated character sprites and supports both mobile and PC platforms.

The full version is planned to include six individual routes: Yanami, Lemon, Komari, Kaju, Tiara, and Shikiya. The total scenario length is expected to be about 1,000,000 Chinese characters.

The development team will not open any crowdfunding campaign, and the completed game will not require paid access. No AI was involved in the production of the game; it is being made entirely through the team's passion and voluntary effort. The only reward the team hopes for is that players who enjoy the game follow them and support their posts with likes, comments, and shares. This matters a lot to them. The team has not abandoned the project; they are simply focused on making the game.
